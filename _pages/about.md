## A few things I've done

Last summer I interned at **Changjiang Securities**, where I spent three months 
deep in China's convertible bond market — tracking 26 bank CBs, building 
databases from scratch, and figuring out why valuations move the way they do. 
It was tedious, rigorous, and genuinely fun.

Outside of work, I've built projects I'm proud of: a **DCF model** for the 
Amazon–Whole Foods deal, a **CAPM beta study** across macro cycles, and a 
**China-US statistical arbitrage strategy** that actually held up in backtesting. 
Most recently, I competed in a Kaggle competition on S&P 500 dynamic allocation 
and hit a **Sharpe ratio of 0.714** on the validation set.
